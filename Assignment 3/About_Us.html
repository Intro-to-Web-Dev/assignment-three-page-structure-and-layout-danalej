Coffee Bench is a site designed to connect you with an ever expanding network of coffee shop suggestions.

These suggestions are curated for you based on a network provided by shops in your area.

Coffee Bench is determined to help users have the best coffee experience no matter where they are.

Coffee Bench sets out with the goal in mind of creating a comprehensive search tool for third wave coffee shops and roasters.
The approach is to compile suggestions from owners, baristas, and roasters across the nation to create a vast network allowing you to search
for a shop tailored to your preferences based on what you are looking for from your coffee experience.
